About Us

CodeForm3D is a product development and innovation company dedicated to turning ideas into reality through expert 3D design, engineering, and rapid prototyping. We offer a complete end-to-end solution for product creation—covering everything from 3D modelling,electronic design, and software engineering to mechanical development and system integration.

We often begin our journey with off-the-shelf components to accelerate development and reduce initial costs. This allows us to rapidly prototype and validate core functionality. As the project evolves, we explore opportunities for bespoke design—tailoring hardware, software, and mechanical elements to meet specific performance, aesthetic, or integration requirements.

At CodeForm3D, we support development across Technology Readiness Levels(TRL) 1 to 9, guiding ideas from initial research through to fully deployable, market-ready solutions.
